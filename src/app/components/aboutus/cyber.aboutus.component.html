What is CyberGen ?

CyberGen is a revolutionary NFT project that allows you to become an owner in a decentralised company.

CyberGen runs the CyberShop, which is owned by the NFT holders, meaning that revenue generated from the online store goes directly into the treasury that is owned and controlled by the community.
